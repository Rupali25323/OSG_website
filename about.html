About Us

OM Shree Geo Tech Pvt. Ltd., operating under the name Shree Geotech & Engineering Services (SGES), has significantly expanded its capabilities in the field of Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) and Research Services since August 2021.

At SGES, we employ a team of highly skilled experts and technicians specializing in a wide range of services, including Pile Load Testing, Anchoring & Grouting, Non-Destructive Testing, Soil Testing, and many other geotechnical assessments.

Our commitment to responsiveness, flexibility, quality, and innovation reflects the high standards our clients expect — and deserve.

Our Vision

To be market leader in Non-Destructive Testing and to grow & to aspire towards complete customer satisfaction through quality service at highest standard to all our customers.

Quality Policy

OSG is committed to continual improvement of processes & services to achieve ongoing customer satisfaction. Consistently comply to ensure quality testing services, of the Quality Management System.

Service / Testing

OSG has a wide range of testing facilities for performing the Physical and Non-Destructive test & Geotechnical investigation under controlled environmental condition in accordance with the BIS & other International Standard.
